Privacy Guarantees

Rewind is designed to keep your data private. Everything stays safe on your own computer:

  • ✓ 100% Private: Every video title, channel, and watch time is saved strictly inside this browser on your computer. No cloud or external servers are used.
  • ✓ No Tracking: No analytical tracking or logging tools are included. What you watch is completely private.
  • ✓ Safe Keys: Your setup keys are saved locally and sent directly to Google. No third-party servers see them.
  • ✓ Total Ownership: You can download your history or wipe it completely in one click.
